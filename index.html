Chef Spotlight

Chef Isaac Uchenna

Head of Culinary & Restaurants

A passionate Nigerian culinary expert with over 10 years of experience crafting authentic soups and local dishes. Chef Isaac brings the warmth of traditional home cooking to every bowl he creates. From rich egusi to slow-cooked banga soup, his recipes are rooted in culture, flavour, and a deep love for Nigerian food. Now partnered with Native Pot, he connects directly with food lovers who appreciate real, homemade taste.

Fatima Audu

Artisanal Soup Specialist

For Ayomide, cooking is a form of storytelling. With a rare mastery of indigenous spices and slow-simmering techniques, she brings the authentic "grandma's kitchen" feel to the busy professional. Whether it's her signature Fisherman’s Soup or perfectly pounded yam, Ayomide focuses on the soul of the dish, ensuring Native Pot customers enjoy a true taste of home, no matter where they are.

Ayomide Benjamin

Master of Fusion & Spices

Ayomide is a visionary chef dedicated to elevating West African cuisine. Known for her meticulous sourcing of local farm-fresh ingredients, she specializes in creating bold, aromatic flavors that bridge the gap between street-food nostalgia and fine dining. Her partnership with Native Pot ensures that every meal delivered is not just food, but a premium culinary experience designed for the modern palate.
